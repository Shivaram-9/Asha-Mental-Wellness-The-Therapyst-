About Asha

19+ years of compassionate care and transformation

🧠
CCI Registered
M.A. Clinical Psychology

Asha Suhasini Raja G is a globally affiliated Senior Mental Wellness Consultant and Psychotherapist with over 19 years of dedicated experience in transforming lives and fostering emotional well-being.

Asha’s work is defined by a compassionate approach, scientific expertise, and an unwavering commitment to empowering individuals to lead balanced, meaningful lives through emotional regulation. Her qualifications include a Bachelor’s in Psychology (specialising in Child Psychology), a Master’s in Clinical Psychology and Psychotherapy from SNDT University, Mumbai, and a Post-Graduation Diploma in Family and Marital Therapy. She is a registered member of the Counsellors Council of India (CRN9047214).

Her career began at Bandra Family Court, Mumbai (2003) as an Assistant Psychotherapist preparing unbiased psychological reports to assist judges and couples in divorce, reconciliation, and child custody cases. Over her career she has combined strong academic foundations with extensive practical experience across clinical, educational and corporate settings.
